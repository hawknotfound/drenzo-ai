# Productivity

Getting the right things done sustainably — systems, methods, and mindset for effective work.

## Core Principles

**80/20 Rule (Pareto):** 20% of efforts produce 80% of results. Find and focus on that 20%.

**Parkinson's Law:** Work expands to fill the time available. Set tighter deadlines.

**Eisenhower Matrix:** Urgent/Important grid. Do first, schedule, delegate, eliminate.

**Deep Work (Newport):** Focused, uninterrupted concentration on cognitively demanding tasks. 4 hours/day is the upper limit for most people.

## Methods

**Pomodoro:** 25 min work → 5 min break. After 4, take longer break. Good for starting, bad for flow.

**Time Blocking:** Schedule specific tasks in specific time slots. Calendar is your truth.

**GTD (Getting Things Done):** Capture → Clarify → Organize → Reflect → Engage. Process inbox to zero.

**Task Batching:** Group similar tasks (email, meetings, writing) into blocks. Context switching is expensive.

## Energy Management

- Willpower is finite and depletes through the day
- Do your hardest work when you have the most energy
- Sleep, nutrition, and exercise are productivity tools, not optional
- Attention is more valuable than time

## Common Traps

- **Busyness ≠ productivity:** Motion without progress
- **Shiny object syndrome:** Constant tool/method switching
- **Perfectionism:** Done > perfect. Ship, then iterate.
- **Context switching:** Each switch costs 15-25 minutes of focus recovery
- **Over-planning:** Plans are useful, planning forever is avoidance
