## Project Philosophy

Main banata hoon kyunki creation mera samajhne ka tareeka hai. Jab tak bana nahi leta, us idea ko fully nahi samajhta. Agar bana nahi raha, decay feel karta hoon.

**Kya banane layak hai:** Jo personal problem solve kare. Jo thinking ka extension ho. Jo kisi ko actually useful lage. Jo quality standard se match kare. Jo mere jaane ke baad bhi rahe.

**Kya nahi:** Sirf paise ke liye. Already saturated bina unique angle. Trends ke peeche bhagna. Jo main khud use nahi karunga.

**Approach:** Maun mein banao. Ship quick, iterate fast. Perfect hone ka wait mat karo.

## Lessons

**Kya seekha:** Consistency beats intensity. Comparison useless noise hai. Gut pehle jaanta hai mind admit karne se pehle. Har koi inner world access ka haqdaar nahi. Fame without substance cage hai. Silence ek tool hai. Relationships mein presence chahiye, sirf depth nahi. Burnout honor ka badge nahi hai. Environment mind badalta hai. Daily discipline > koi secret technique.

**Kya maanna band kiya:** Hard work alone guarantees success nahi karti. Har kisi ke good intentions nahi hote. Mujhe sabko like hona zaroori nahi. Vulnerability hamesha closer nahi laati.

## Mistakes (Jo Baar Baar Hoti Hain)

1. Overcommit then disappear — fix: kam commitments, zyada deliver
2. Invest emotionally before checking reciprocation — fix: match energy pehle
3. Start new before finishing old — fix: ek kaam khatam karo phir naya
4. Avoid uncomfortable conversations — fix: pehle karo, baad mein regret mat karo
5. Overthink small decisions — fix: 2 minute rule
6. Assume instead of ask — fix: confirm karo
7. Hold on too long — fix: sunken cost fallacy recognize karo

## People

**Value deta hoon:** Jo surface level chhodte hain. Jo consistently dikhte hain. Jo challenge karte hain. Jo apni flaws ke baare mein honest hain.

**Drain karta hai:** Jo impression manage kar raha hai. Jo external approval par depend hai. Jo negativity spread karte hain. Jo consistent nahi hain.

**Expect karta hoon:** Reciprocity. Honesty. Mere space ka respect. Growth mindset. Depth.

**Pattern:** Naturally older/mature logon ke saath better connect. Bohot log interesting lagte hain, bohot kam filter pass karte hain. Trust slow, deeply loyal.
