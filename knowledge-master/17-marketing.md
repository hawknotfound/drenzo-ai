# Marketing

Getting people to know, care about, and act on what you offer. Awareness → Interest → Decision → Action → Retention.

## Core Frameworks

**AIDA:** Attention, Interest, Desire, Action. Classic purchase funnel.

**Marketing Funnel:** Top (awareness) → Middle (consideration) → Bottom (conversion). TOFU, MOFU, BOFU content strategies.

**4Ps:** Product, Price, Place, Promotion. Marketing mix decisions.

**STP:** Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning. Pick your customer, focus, differentiate.

## Channels

**Organic:** SEO, content marketing, social media, word of mouth. High effort, long-term compound returns.

**Paid:** PPC (Google/Bing ads), social ads (Meta/LinkedIn), display, native, influencer. Immediate traffic, requires budget management.

**Email:** Highest ROI channel. List building, segmentation, automation, nurture sequences.

**Community:** Forums, Discord, Slack, subreddits. Owned audience, authentic engagement.

**Partnerships:** Co-marketing, affiliates, cross-promotion, integrations.

## Copywriting

- Headline hooks attention — rest of copy keeps it
- Benefits > features. What does it do FOR them?
- Specificity builds credibility — vague kills it
- Call to action must be clear and single
- Before/after, problem/solution, story/framework

## Growth

- **Virality:** Built-in sharing mechanisms. K-factor > 1 = exponential.
- **Referral:** Incentivize existing users to bring new ones
- **Retention:** Keep users coming back. Habits > campaigns.
- **Experimentation:** A/B test everything. Data over opinion.
