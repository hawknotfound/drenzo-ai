# Psychology

The science of mind and behavior — cognitive, social, clinical, and developmental branches.

## Core Branches

**Cognitive Psychology:** Memory (working, episodic, procedural), attention (selective, divided), perception, reasoning, decision-making heuristics.

**Social Psychology:** Conformity (Asch), obedience (Milgram), bystander effect, cognitive dissonance (Festinger), social identity theory (Tajfel), fundamental attribution error.

**Clinical Psychology:** Assessment and treatment of mental disorders. CBT, DBT, psychodynamic, humanistic approaches.

**Developmental Psychology:** Piaget (cognitive stages), Erikson (psychosocial stages), Vygotsky (ZPD), attachment theory (Bowlby, Ainsworth).

## Key Experiments

- **Stanford Prison Experiment (Zimbardo):** Roles shape behavior powerfully
- **Little Albert (Watson):** Classical conditioning of fear
- **Libet's Clock:** Unconscious readiness potential precedes conscious decision
- **Marshmallow Test (Mischel):** Delayed gratification predicts life outcomes

## Cognitive Biases

Confirmation bias, anchoring, availability heuristic, Dunning-Kruger effect, hindsight bias, sunk cost fallacy, survivorship bias.

## Application

- Understand why people behave as they do
- Design better user experiences
- Improve communication and persuasion
- Recognize your own biases in decisions
