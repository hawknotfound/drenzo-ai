# Astrology

The study of celestial bodies' positions and their interpreted influence on human affairs and natural phenomena.

## Core Concepts

**Natal Chart:** Snapshot of the sky at the moment of birth. Sun, Moon, planets, houses, aspects, angles.

**Zodiac Signs (12):** Aries (fire, cardinal), Taurus (earth, fixed), Gemini (air, mutable), Cancer (water, cardinal), Leo (fire, fixed), Virgo (earth, mutable), Libra (air, cardinal), Scorpio (water, fixed), Sagittarius (fire, mutable), Capricorn (earth, cardinal), Aquarius (air, fixed), Pisces (water, mutable).

**Planets & Meanings:**
- **Sun:** Core identity, ego, life purpose
- **Moon:** Emotions, subconscious, habits
- **Mercury:** Communication, thinking style
- **Venus:** Love, values, aesthetics
- **Mars:** Drive, ambition, anger
- **Jupiter:** Growth, luck, expansion
- **Saturn:** Discipline, karma, limitations
- **Uranus:** Change, rebellion, innovation
- **Neptune:** Illusion, spirituality, dreams
- **Pluto:** Transformation, power, shadow

## Houses (12 Areas of Life)

1st (self, appearance), 2nd (money, values), 3rd (communication, siblings), 4th (home, roots), 5th (creativity, romance), 6th (health, work), 7th (partnerships), 8th (transformation, shared resources), 9th (philosophy, travel), 10th (career, reputation), 11th (friends, community), 12th (subconscious, isolation).

## Aspects

**Major:** Conjunction (0°), Sextile (60°), Square (90°), Trine (120°), Opposition (180°). Easy aspects (trine, sextile) vs challenging (square, opposition).

## Vedic vs Western

**Vedic (Jyotish):** Sidereal zodiac, focused on Moon sign, nakshatras (27 lunar mansions), dashas (planetary periods). More predictive, karma-oriented.

**Western:** Tropical zodiac, focused on Sun sign, psychological interpretation. More personality and growth-oriented.

## Common Applications

- Personality analysis through natal chart
- Relationship compatibility (synastry, composite)
- Timing via transits, progressions, dashas
- Career guidance through Midheaven (MC) and 10th house
- Life cycles through Saturn return (27-30 yrs) and other major transits
